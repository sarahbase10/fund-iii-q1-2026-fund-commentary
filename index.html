Round Details

Slide — Series B at $500M post, 4x markup to our entry

Slide, the MSP-focused BCDR (backup, continuity, disaster recovery) platform where Base10 led the Series A, signed a $70M Series B led by General Catalyst on March 10 at a $500M post-money valuation, a 4x markup to our Series A entry. The business scaled from $0 to $6.5M CARR in its first 9 months of selling and is tracking to cross $10M CARR before it laps the first anniversary of going to market. Base10's pro-rata allocation is $7.8M and we are exercising it in full.

Loop AI — Series A at $14M, announced February 2

Loop AI announced a $14M Series A led by NYCA at [Loop post $TK] post-money. Loop is our freight-claims and parcel-recovery AI platform and still one of the clearest examples in the book of the real-economy-agent thesis: the business went $0 to $4M to $14M revenue in 24 months across popular restaurant chains including Lazy Dog and Starbird. Base10 led the seed and participated pro-rata in the Series A.

Fund III impact: Loop AI is now marked at 1.9x Gross MOIC.

Greenboard — Series A at $100M post, 4x markup to our entry

Greenboard, the AI-native securities compliance platform where Base10 led the seed in March 2024, closed its $15.5M Series A led by Base10 at a $100M post-money valuation in September 2025, a 4x markup to our $25M-post seed entry. Our seed SAFEs converted at the Series A.

The business grew roughly 7x in 2025, from $400K ARR at the start of the year to $2.8M signed ARR by year-end and crossed $3M ARR in January 2026. Q1 2026 was Greenboard's first $1M quarter, ending at $3.8M signed ARR against a $4M plan, with a 2026 target of $10M live ARR. Greenboard now serves 500+ financial institutions with 99%+ customer retention.

Fund III impact: Greenboard is now marked at 1.4x Gross MOIC.

Phonely

Series A · Base10-led · Dec 2025

AI voice automation platform for real-economy enterprises — the platform lets businesses build voice agents that handle inbound and outbound calls to qualify customers, schedule appointments, and scale to millions of calls. Use cases today span lead qualification, answering services, appointment scheduling, and AI receptionists in healthcare, home services, and adjacent real-economy verticals.

Fund III led the Series A with a $12M check of a $15M round at a $115M post-money valuation (10.4% ownership).

Amera

Seed · Base10-led · Aug 2025

Amera is automating claims processing workflows for health insurance payors. The team is treating the source of the problem (claims data automation at the payor) rather than its symptoms — relevant given how much is stuck in this part of healthcare. Part of our tighter, more intentional YC batch approach (20–25 companies evaluated with 24-hour decisions, rather than 50+).

Fund III led the YC seed round with a $2.5M SAFE at $30M post-money.

Denki

Seed · Co-led · Aug 2025

Denki is automating internal audit workflows (planning, testing, documentation) for financial-regulatory compliance, augmenting in-house audit teams and working inside existing systems like Auditboard and Workiva.

Original Fund III investment was $1.25M at a $25M post-money cap (5% ownership) via SAFE. The founder subsequently offered us an additional $250K allocation to bring us to $1.5M / 6% ownership, which we took; Shine Capital co-leads at the same size.
